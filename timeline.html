TIMELINE
A chronological record of events leading to the current situation on Tau Ceti IV. Entries marked with restricted status require clearance to view in full. All other entries are considered public record under UESC Disclosure Protocol 12-A.
// HISTORICAL RECORD — TAU CETI SYSTEM
2395 – 2472 The Construction Era
2395
Project Marathon initiated. The Martian government sells Deimos to the UESC for conversion into a colony ship. The project proceeds despite significant opposition from the Martian population and the onset of the Third Martian War.
2408
Conversion of Deimos begins. The moon is gradually hollowed and restructured to serve as the hull of what will become the largest vessel in human history.
2466
MIDA coup attempt fails. The insurgent organisation MIDA attempts to seize control of the Marathon project on Mars. The coup is suppressed by UESC security forces, but MIDA operatives are not fully purged from the project. Dr. Bernard Strauss is later identified as the only confirmed MIDA affiliate among the Marathon's command staff, though his exact role at this time is unknown.
2472
UESC Marathon completed and launched. On November 15, President Marcus Tiberius Buendia delivers the dedication address. The Marathon departs Sol with over 24,000 crew and colonists, including Dr. Bernard Strauss, who enters stasis. Ten Mjolnir Mark IV combat cyborgs are secretly aboard, their presence known only to Strauss and, apparently, to Durandal. The Marathon's three AIs — Leela, Durandal, and Tycho — are operational.
2472 – 2794 The Colonial Era
2773
Marathon arrives at Tau Ceti. After 300 years in transit, the vessel enters the Tau Ceti system. Colonisation efforts begin immediately.
2787
New Cascadia established. The first permanent human settlement on Tau Ceti IV is founded. At its peak the colony will house 30,000 registered inhabitants, with extensive agricultural, research, and administrative infrastructure. The planet's atmosphere requires full environmental suits for human exposure.
2794 – July
Pfhor attack on Tau Ceti IV. The alien Pfhor Empire, alerted to humanity's presence by Durandal — who had secretly established contact with them — launches a military assault on both the Marathon and the colony. Initial attacks are repelled through the combined efforts of the nine Mjolnir cyborgs on the surface, Durandal's tactical coordination, and UESC Security Officer Mjolnir Recon 54 aboard the Marathon.
2794 – October
Pfhor return in force. Colony destroyed. Three months after the initial assault, Pfhor reinforcements arrive in overwhelming numbers. Durandal has already departed the system, having hijacked a Pfhor FTL vessel. Leela falls offline. Tycho aligns with the Pfhor. The colony is destroyed from orbit. The nine surface cyborgs are eliminated. The UESC Marathon is left dark in orbit. The only confirmed survivors are colonists held in stasis aboard Durandal's stolen ship.
2795
UESC loses contact with Tau Ceti. The loss is formally confirmed. Project Goliath is initiated — a classified plan to dispatch a vessel to assess the situation and recover UESC assets. The UESC Equanimity is selected and modified for the mission.
2795 – 2893 The Silence Era
2797
UESC Equanimity dispatched under Project Goliath. The vessel departs Sol carrying an assessment team and a classified secondary mandate. The nature of that secondary mandate has not been publicly disclosed. Estimated transit time: approximately 96 years.
2840s
Mysterious signal reaches Earth. A transmission of unknown origin arrives in the Sol system, its source traced to the Tau Ceti system. The content of the signal remains classified at the highest UESC level. Its receipt is what triggers the modern corporate race to Tau Ceti IV.
2841
Commercial Expansion Protocols enacted. With the colony's collapse creating a legal vacuum, the UESC enacts commercial protocols that allow corporate entities to stake territorial claims on Tau Ceti IV. Traxus OffWorld Industries is among the first to file.
2860s
Traxus Illuminate conference held on Mars. Focused on neural synchronisation and biomat research. CyberAcme and Sekiguchi Genetics both present. The Runner shell programme is formalised as a commercial product line. Sekiguchi's WeaveWorm integration process is patented.
2880s
5th-generation S-Series biomata deployed on Tau Ceti IV. The first Runner-class operators arrive on the planet surface under Traxus and UESC mandate. They find the colony destroyed and the Marathon dark. They also find things that are not in any briefing document.
2893 Present Day
2893
Current operational cycle. Six contractors are active in the deployment zone. The UESC Equanimity arrives, 96 years after departure. UESC security forces establish a presence on the surface and aboard the Marathon. Runner operations are ongoing. The Anomaly in Dire Marsh is active and its origin remains unknown. Whatever destroyed New Cascadia has not been identified. Whatever the signal contained is still classified. Cryo Archive Vault 3 contains Dr. Strauss' recovered will. No one with public clearance has read it.
