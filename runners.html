// CLASSIFICATION: PUBLIC // BIO-CYBERNETIC SHELL REGISTRY
RUNNER
SHELLS
Runners are humans who have abandoned their biological bodies in favour of bio-cybernetic shells — specialised frames printed by CyberAcme and other licensed manufacturers. Each shell classification is purpose-built for a distinct operational profile. Six shells are active for the current deployment cycle.
// ACTIVE SHELL CLASSIFICATIONS — CYCLE 2893
RUNNER CLASS //
BLACKBIRD
Scout / Intelligence
Blackbird shells are built for information dominance. A Blackbird team that communicates effectively is rarely surprised. Their detection systems make them invaluable in multi-team environments where knowing where threats are before they arrive is the difference between extraction and termination.
PRIME
Echo Pulse
Releases a barrage of sonar pulses, revealing the location of all nearby hostiles.
TACTICAL
Tracker Drone
Deploys a mechanised microbot that tracks down nearby hostiles and detonates on contact, overheating targets.
TRAIT
Interrogation
Performing a finisher on a downed Runner pings their entire crew. Automatically warns you when pinged by a hostile.
TRAIT
Stalker Protocol
Breaking an enemy's shield leaves a lingering holographic trail, allowing you to track their movement.
RUNNER CLASS //
GLITCH
Mobility / Disruption
Glitch shells prioritise movement speed and positional advantage above all else. A Glitch who understands their environment is nearly impossible to pin down. Their disruption capabilities allow them to destabilise engagements in ways that confuse and scatter opponents before a conventional firefight begins.
PRIME
Amplify
Supercharges the shell's movement systems, granting enhanced speed and double-jump capability for a short duration.
TACTICAL
Disruptor
Deploys an electromagnetic pulse device that disrupts enemy systems and ability usage in an area.
TRAIT
Microjets
Passive jet-assisted movement system that enables extended air time and rapid directional changes.
TRAIT
Power Slide
Allows high-speed sliding manoeuvres that can be chained into jumps, maintaining momentum.
RUNNER CLASS //
LOCUS
Assault / Area Denial
Locus shells are blunt instruments. They are built for Runners who have decided that the most effective strategy is to move forward and eliminate everything in their path. Their offensive capabilities are formidable but come at the cost of defensive flexibility. A Locus who stops moving is a Locus who dies.
PRIME
Search and Destroy
Activates shoulder-mounted missile launchers that fire homing projectiles, immobilising and damaging targets on impact.
TACTICAL
Riot Barricade
Deploys a reinforced directional barrier that blocks incoming fire and can be used for aggressive pushes.
TRAIT
Thruster
A short-range burst propulsion system allowing rapid directional repositioning in combat.
TRAIT
Tactical Sprint
Enhanced sprint capability that reduces incoming damage during movement and improves collision resistance.
RUNNER CLASS //
VOID
Stealth / Evasion
Void shells do not win fights by engaging them. They win by choosing when an engagement happens, where it happens, and on whose terms. The most effective Void operators are those who understand that invisibility is not simply the absence of being seen — it is the presence of uncertainty in an opponent's mind.
PRIME
Smoke Screen
Deploys a wide-radius chemical smoke cloud that obscures vision and disrupts targeting systems.
TACTICAL
Active Camo
Activates full optical camouflage, rendering the shell nearly invisible for a short duration.
TRAIT
Shadow Dive
A rapid concealed movement technique that allows repositioning without breaking stealth.
TRAIT
Shroud
Passively reduces the shell's heat and acoustic signature, making detection significantly harder at range.
RUNNER CLASS //
THIEF
Scavenge / Acquisition
Thief shells are optimised for one purpose: extracting value from hostile environments faster than anyone else. Whether the target is a weapon cache, a data terminal, or loot on a downed Runner, Thief capabilities are built to acquire it quickly and disappear before the consequences arrive. Thief operators tend to be people who are very good at knowing when to run.
PRIME
Loot Sense
Briefly reveals the location of all lootable containers and dropped items within a wide radius.
TACTICAL
Quick Hands
Dramatically accelerates interaction speed with containers, terminals, and downed Runners for a short duration.
TRAIT
Lightweight
Reduced shell mass allows faster base movement and quieter footstep audio signature.
TRAIT
Finders Keepers
Increases the loot quality tier of items found in containers during a run.
RUNNER CLASS //
TRIAGE
Support / Recovery
Triage shells are the only Runner class specifically designed with another Runner's survival in mind. In a zone where every team member represents an irreplaceable asset, a Triage operator's ability to maintain crew functionality under fire is a force multiplier that changes the calculus of every engagement. Teams without Triage can win runs. Teams with a skilled Triage operator tend to survive them.
PRIME
Healing Drone
Deploys a mobile medical drone that automatically administers repair protocols to nearby allied Runners.
TACTICAL
Emergency Extract
Rapidly stabilises a critically damaged allied Runner and boosts their immediate recovery rate.
TRAIT
Field Surgeon
Increases the effectiveness of all healing items used by you and nearby allies.
TRAIT
Resilience
Passively reduces damage taken by allies within close proximity of the Triage shell.
// SPECIAL CLASSIFICATION: ROOK
Rook is not a standard shell. It is a contingency deployment option — a stripped-down frame issued to operators who have lost their primary shell and need to re-enter the field immediately. Rook deployments are solo, mid-match, and loadout-locked. No offensive abilities. No upgrade path. Rook is a last resort, not a strategy. Its primary function is scavenging enough resources to fund a proper re-entry on the next run. Rook is available on Perimeter, Dire Marsh, and Outpost only. It cannot be selected for Cryo Archive operations.
