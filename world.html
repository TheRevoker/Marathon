TAU CETI
IV
Humanity's first extrasolar colony. A graveyard of 30,000 missing souls. The most valuable and dangerous piece of real estate in the known universe.
// PLANETARY OVERVIEW

Tau Ceti IV orbits a G-type star located 11.9 light-years from Sol in the constellation Cetus. It is the fourth planet in its system and the site of humanity's first successful extrasolar settlement, established in 2787 under the designation New Cascadia.

At its peak, the colony supported a population of 30,000 human settlers, operating from a network of research facilities, agricultural stations, and urban infrastructure modules. The UESC Marathon, the colony ship that carried the original settlers, orbited the planet as a platform of last resort — a symbol of human ambition and institutional pride.

That symbol now hangs dark and silent in low orbit. Its 30,000 crew and colonists are gone. No distress signal was ever transmitted. No evacuation was recorded. The only thing left is silence, ruins, and a signal that reached Earth decades later — a signal no one has been able to explain.

UESC INTERNAL NOTICE // The exact circumstances of the colony's disappearance remain under active investigation. Public disclosure of findings is suspended pending review by the Office of Colonial Affairs. Violation of this order constitutes a Class IV infraction.

In the years following the disappearance, corporate interests descended on Tau Ceti IV with remarkable speed. The legal vacuum left by the colony's collapse allowed megacorporations to stake territorial claims under the Commercial Expansion Protocols of 2841. Six major contractors now operate in the deployment zone, each pursuing their own interpretation of what happened — and what it is worth.

// ACTIVE DEPLOYMENT ZONES
ZONE 01 //
Perimeter
THREAT: ELEVATED
The outermost boundary of the original New Cascadia settlement. More spread out than inner zones, with additional concealment opportunities. UESC forces patrol the area, attempting to reconstruct what happened during the colony's final hours. Recommended as initial insertion point for newly activated Runners. Death remains a constant risk.
ZONE 02 //
Dire Marsh
THREAT: SEVERE
A region bearing visible signs of catastrophic events. The Anomaly — a massive biological or geological entity of unknown origin — erupted from the ground here and remains active. Structural damage across all infrastructure. Hostile organism density is significantly elevated. The source of the repeating encoded signal identified in recent dispatches.
ZONE 03 //
Outpost
THREAT: SEVERE
A former UESC security installation converted into a contested resource hub. Multiple faction interests compete openly here. The highest concentration of lootable technology caches on the planet surface. Runner-on-Runner engagement rates are the highest of any surface zone. Intelligence suggests Sekiguchi maintains a covert research operation somewhere within its boundaries.
ZONE 04 //
Cryo Archive
THREAT: EXTREME
Aboard the UESC Marathon itself. Seven frozen vaults, each more fortified than the last, containing artifacts and data from the colony's final days. The highest tier of UESC security units operate here in concentrations not found elsewhere. Cramped corridors make every encounter a close-quarters engagement. This is where answers live — if you can survive long enough to find them.
